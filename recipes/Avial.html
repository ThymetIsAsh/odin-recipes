Avial

Description

Avial is a mixed vegetable curry from Kerala, cooked with coconut, green chilies, and cumin. It is flavored with yogurt and curry leaves, creating a creamy and aromatic dish that pairs well with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato, yam)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables into bite-sized pieces and cook until tender.
  2. Grind grated coconut, green chilies, and cumin seeds into a paste.
  3. Add the coconut paste to the cooked vegetables and mix well.
  4. Add yogurt and cook for a few minutes until heated through.
  5. Garnish with curry leaves and serve with rice.
