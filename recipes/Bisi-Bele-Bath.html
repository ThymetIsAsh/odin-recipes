Bisi Bele Bath

Description

Bisi Bele Bath is a traditional dish from Karnataka, featuring a spicy, tangy, and slightly sweet mixture of rice, toor dal, and vegetables. It is cooked with a special spice blend and tamarind, creating a rich and hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 2 tablespoons Bisi Bele Bath powder
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook rice and toor dal together in a pressure cooker.
  2. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté the mixed vegetables.
  3. Add Bisi Bele Bath powder and tamarind paste to the vegetables, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the cooked rice and dal mixture to the vegetables, and mix well.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until well combined.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
