Chicken Chettinad

Description

Chicken Chettinad is a spicy and flavorful curry from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu. It features marinated chicken cooked with a blend of freshly ground spices, coconut, tomatoes, and onions, resulting in a rich and aromatic dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons Chettinad masala
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with salt and a portion of the Chettinad masala.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is done.
  6. Add the grated coconut and remaining Chettinad masala. Mix well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
